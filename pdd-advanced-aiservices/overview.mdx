# PDD Advanced AIServices

**Version:** 2.0.0 | **Framework:** QBCore, QBX, ESX | **Author:** PhatDaddy Development LLC

PDD AI Services dispatches AI-driven city service units when real players aren't on duty. When no EMS, mechanic, or taxi player is available, an AI unit responds instead — fully animated, fully configurable, no player required.

Everything is managed through an in-game Samsung Galaxy Tab S-style NUI tablet. No config file editing required for day-to-day operations.

## Services

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="EMS" icon="truck-medical">
    Ground ambulance, air rescue with zipline rappel, and underwater sub rescue. Revives downed players with configurable health thresholds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mechanic" icon="wrench">
    On-site repair truck, fuel delivery, marine mechanic, and helicopter airlift. Flat-rate or vehicle-value-based pricing. Auto-dispatches tow to active player shops when available.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transport" icon="car">
    Standard taxi or luxury limo with distance-based pricing and server-side fare clamping.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Police Impound" icon="shield">
    LEO-only tab. Dispatches a tow unit and opens a full impound form on arrival — price, hold time, authorization, and notes.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Key Features

- **Pager alert system** — notifies real on-duty workers when AI dispatches so they can take over
- **Fast Travel** — skip AI travel time with a configurable key (default: `E`)
- **In-tablet admin panel** — live toggle and full config for all services, no file editing required
- **Server-authoritative pricing** — client-sent prices are never trusted
- **Jail detection** — blocks service access for incarcerated players
- **Cancel refund system** — optional refund on cancel with anti-abuse cooldown
